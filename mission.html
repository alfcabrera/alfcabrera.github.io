Mission Statement

Our goal is to provide the highest quality products through excellence in design and solutions.

Through our culture, our drive and the expertise of each individual employee, we are uniquely positioned to provide best-in-class product and services to a global customer base.

Core Values

Safety

Creating a workplace focused on the health and well-being of the employees, the environment, our customers and the communities where we do business.

Passion

Dedication to our mission, clients, our company, the environment and each other.

Transparency

Exercising sound business judgment in all our decisions and actions in order to accomplish our mission.

Teamwork

Working together to foster creativity and leveraging individual strengths to reach common goals and objectives.

Respect

Being honest and forthright in everything we do; while understanding and valuing the contributions and priorities of others.

Corporate Responsibility

Environmental Management

DM5I expects it’s supplier to manage their operations reponsibly in relation to environmental risk and impact, to adopt aprecautionary approach and to have a life cycle perspective in their business operations

Integrity

DM5I encourage it’s suppliers to conduct business in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations and adhere to internationally agreed standards os business ethics.

Safety

Suppliers shall ensure a safe and healthy workplace or any other location where work is performed, all processes shall be preceded by and be based on adequate risk management.

Corporate social Responsability

Suppliers shall respect the rights, interests, and development aspirations of affected communities during significant changes of supplier’s normal operations.
