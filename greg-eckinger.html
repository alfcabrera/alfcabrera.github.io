Vice President of Customer Solutions

I am the Vice President of Customer Solutions for Airlink Distribution, focused on tailoring programs for IT asset recovery that match the needs of our customers. Our focus is to maximize the value of your IT assets while providing administrative and data integration support to minimize your labor burden. I am an expert at leading high-performing global teams and scaling solutions to optimize operations; delivering best cost, quality, speed, and governance. I love solving complex problems and I’m fortunate to be in a position and industry with no shortage of them.
