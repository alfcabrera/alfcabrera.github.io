Jael Arias

Sr. Carrier Delivery Manager

As a Senior Carrier Delivery Manager for Airlink Distribution, Jael has helped the company to develop the relationships with our customers keeping us as a top vendor for each of them. His skills in financial analysis (budgeting, cash flow, P&L, etc.), operations, and project management, have been a constant source of value creation not only for our company but for our customers and suppliers.

In his career as a Project and Program Manager in the electronics (reverse logistics, remanufacturing) industry, he has managed important accounts such as AT&T, Asurion, Ingram Micro, and others. For all these customers with Jael’s contribution, Airlink Distribution has been a top performer in the following industry KPIs: TAT (Turnaround Time), RMAs, Repair Yield, and Invoice Reconciliation.

Jael holds a university degree in industrial engineering and master’s degrees in Renewable Energies from EOI Business School in Madrid and an MBA from ENAE Business School in Murcia (both in Spain).
