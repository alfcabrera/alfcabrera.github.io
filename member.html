Junior Veras

Managing Director DR

Junior Veras is a Managing Director at Airlink Distribution. With over 12 years of experience in the electronic remanufacturing industry, Junior has mastered skills like remanufacturing, engineering, operations, profit and loss management, facilities management, cost efficiency and development, lean manufacturing, and continuous improvement.

Junior earned his bachelor’s degree in Industrial Engineering and his technical expert degree in Digital Electronics from ITESA Institute. His career began at Ace Wireless Group in 2009 as an NPI Technical before he advanced to different leadership positions. By 2010, he moved to China to learn more about cell phone refurbishment and radio frequency with the latest wideband communication tester.

Junior returned to the Dominican Republic and joined Encore Repair Services in 2015 as General Manager. Here, he contributed significantly to the foundation and implementation of the Free Trade Zone, resulting in the growth of over 1,700 employees at the Dominican Republic Site. He has led cell phone remanufacturing processes in Central Europe, the Czech Republic, and now in the Dominican Republic at Airlink Distribution with the intention to grow businesses opportunities in his country.
