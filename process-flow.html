Cosmetic Grading Standard

Level 1

Like New / Deployable
  • No functional defects
  • No visible usage
  • Minimal scratches and blemishes (Up to 2 cosmetic defects on all main surfaces)
  • Battery health (SOH) ≥ 85%

Level 2

Gently Used
  • No functional defects
  • Gentle use visible
  • May have cosmetic defects that do not impact customer experience (Up to 4 cosmetic defects on all main surfaces)
  • Battery health (SOH) ≥ 85%

Level 3

Used
  • No functional defects
  • Major use visible
  • May have cosmetic defects that do not impact customer experience (Unlimited cosmetic defects on all main surfaces)
  • Battery health (SOH) ≥ 75%

Level 4

Heavy Cosmetic Damage
  • No functional defects
  • Excessive damage on multiple surface areas such as broken glass, cracked, bent, etc.
  • No battery health (SOH) requirement
